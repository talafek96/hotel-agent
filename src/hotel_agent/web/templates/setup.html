Import Your Bookings

Upload an Excel file with your hotel bookings. You can also do this later from the Import page.

AI-powered parsing — Your Excel doesn't need exact column names. The AI will do its best to recognize your data. For best results, include columns that resemble: Hotel Name, City, Check-in Date, Check-out Date, Price, Currency, Platform (e.g., Booking.com).
After importing, go to the Bookings tab to review the parsed data. The AI parsing is smart but not perfect — you may need to correct some fields like dates, prices, or room types that were misread.
